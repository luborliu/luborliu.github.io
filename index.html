About Me

I am currently working as software engineer in Seattle, WA. I graduated from Dalhousie University in September 2015 with a master degree in data science. Before coming to U.S, I was a Research Assistant in Institute for Big Data Analytics, under the supervision of Prof.Stan Matwin (Canada Research Chair Tier 1).
I am a self-motivated fast learner with strong team work spirit and great enthusiasm for Data Mining, Machine Learning and Big Data Analytics.
